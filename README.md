## The Problem

Despite 100% completion rates on annual security videos, phishing-related incidents increased by 15% in Q3. Root cause analysis revealed **Compliance Fatigue** — employees knew the rules but lacked the behavioural habit to apply them under high-pressure work conditions.

---

## The Solution

**The Human Firewall Championship** — a 30-day gamified learning ecosystem comprising three interlocking components:

| Component | Approach | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 🏆 Tournament Gamification | Dept-vs-dept leaderboard, badges | Social motivation & habit formation |
| 🥽 AR Just-in-Time Learning | Marker-based office AR (patented approach) | Environmental reinforcement |
| 📱 Header Detective Mini-Game | Daily 2-min mobile challenge | Spaced practice & skill building |

---

## Results

| Kirkpatrick Level | Metric | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 — Reaction | Learner preference vs. video | 92% preferred new format |
| Level 2 — Learning | Post-assessment pass rate | 87% within 30 days |
| Level 3 — Behaviour | Proactive reporting increase | +300% in 60 days |
| Level 4 — Results | Risk mitigation value | ~$1.2M/year estimated |

## Interactive Features

- **Fishbone Diagram** — Click each root cause to reveal the analysis behind the performance gap
- **30-Day Roadmap** — Click each week to explore scaffolded objectives and activities
- **Header Detective Game** — 3-round playable demo; identify phishing indicators in simulated email headers
- **ROI Dashboard** — Animated Chart.js visualisations (click rate trend, reporting volume, old vs. new comparison)
